Our history

Our history dates back to 1973 when our First ofshore Bank opened for business. As regional banks proliferated across the country in the years that followed, a number of other predecessors were born. After a series of mergers at the turn of the 21st century, we formally took on the S.T.B Bank name and established our headquarters in Minnesota. Today, U.S. Bank is the fifth-largest bank in the country, with more than 70,000 employees and $495 billion in assets as of December 31, 2019.

Our Business

Our diverse business mix is fundamental in delivering a consistent, predictable and repeatable financial performance year after year. Our core businesses include Consumer & Business Banking, Corporate & Commercial Banking, Payment Services and Wealth Management & Investment Services. Through our “One U.S. Bank” philosophy, we are able to bring the power of the whole bank to every customer, every single day.
